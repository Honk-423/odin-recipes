Spicy Queso

Description

Made with a variety of delicious peppers and other vegetables, this queso recipe is mouthwateringly good! If you can handle the spice.

Ingredients

Steps

  1. Prepare and sort ingredients.
  2. Melt butter over medium heat in a large saucepan or small pot.
  3. Add onion and cook slowly for three minutes, or until the onion is translucent but not browned.
  4. Add tomato and peppers (excluding the jalapeños) and cook until softened.
  5. Mix the cornstarch with a tablespoon of water. Then add evaporated milk, cheese, and the cornstarch.
  6. Stir then add the spices and jalapeños. Continue to mix until the cheese melts and becomes a somewhat thick sauce.
  7. Stir in two tablespoons of milk to adjust consistency. The dip will thicken when cooled, more milk can be added then to reach preferred consistency.
  8. Remove from heat. Serve warm or at room temperature.
