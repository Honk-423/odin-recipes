Brazilian Lemonade

Description

On a hot day lemonade is the drink of choice for many. Brazilian lemonade is similar to what most are accustomed to, while also bringing a refreshing creaminess with a hint of bitterness.

Ingredients

  • 2 limes
  • 3 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • ice cubes

Steps

  1. Wash limes thoroughly. Cut off the ends and slice into eight wedges.
  2. Place limes in a blender with water, sugar, sweetened condensed milk, and ice; pulse 5 times, or until smooth. Strain through a fine mesh strainer to remove rinds. Serve over ice.
