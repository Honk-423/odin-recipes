Avocado Pizza

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • Chopped bacon
  • Chicken
  • Light mayo
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups chicken breast, cooked, cut into cubes(Cooked with honey and chipotle aioli)
  • Roasted red peppers
  • Chipotle aioli
  • Flatbread

Steps

  1. In a small/medium jar mix olive oil, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, oregano, and chili flakes together. Shake it well, then save the mixture in refrigerator for later.
  2. Pre-heat the oven at 425ºF (220ºC).
  3. Oil spray a flat baking pan.
  4. Spread garlic sauce on flatbread. Top with the shredded mozzarella, cooked and sliced chicken breasts, and sprinkle diced onions.
  5. Bake it in the oven for 25-30 minutes until the cheese melts and the crust become golden brown.
  6. Take the pizza out and top with sliced avocados.
