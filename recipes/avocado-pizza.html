Avocado Pizza

Description

A more convenient method of making avocado pizza, but no less delicious. It's packed to the brim with savory flavors and covered in a creamy aioli sauce.

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Chipotle aioli
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
  • Flatbread
  • Garlic aioli
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups of chicken breasts
  • 1/4 or 1/6 a cup of onion, diced
  • Roasted red peppers
  • Chopped bacon

Steps

  1. Coat chicken breasts in honey and chipotle aioli, then cook it in a small/medium pan. Remove from pan once cooked and cut into cubes.
  2. In a small/medium jar mix olive oil, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, oregano, and chili flakes together. Shake it well, then save the mixture in refrigerator for later.
  3. Pre-heat the oven at 425ºF (220ºC).
  4. Oil spray a flat baking pan.
  5. Spread garlic sauce and garlic aioli on flatbread. Top with the cheeses, cooked chicken breasts, and sprinkle diced onion.
  6. Bake it in the oven for 25-30 minutes until the cheese melts and the crust become golden brown.
  7. Take the pizza out. Top with sliced avocados, roasted red peppers, and bacon.
